Allegations of illegal weapon possession, use or sale can lead to severe federal and state penalties. If you’re facing gun charges in the Brooklyn area or New York, you need an advocate who understands the relevant statutes and constitutional issues. At Law Firm of Camacho & Associates, P.C., I help ensure that my clients’ rights are secured at every stage. Whether you’re accused of violating New York laws on possession or federal regulations on weapons sales, I have the skill and experience to create a strategy designed to achieve your best possible result.

Proven litigator provides strong representation for all types of federal weapons charges

At Law Firm of Camacho & Associates, P.C., I deliver comprehensive legal support for any weapons issue governed by federal law, including:

Determined counselor helps individuals confront alleged violations of New York gun laws

  • Violation of purchase or permitting requirements
  • Aggravation of other criminal charges due to weapon use

No matter the specific circumstances of your case, I have the background to explain your legal situation clearly and the skill to help deliver the best possible result.
