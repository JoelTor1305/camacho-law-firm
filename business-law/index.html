Business Law

Assertive New York Attorney Protects Your Business Interests

Seasoned lawyer provides strong legal support to company owners in the Brooklyn area

The business world can be as risky as it is rewarding, so if you are planning to start your own New York company, expand your current operations, or deal with conflicts, you need to know how to protect yourself and your assets. At the Law Firm of Camacho & Associates, P.C., I take pride in supporting the enterprising individuals who help grow the economy in the Brooklyn area and throughout New York. I am dedicated to helping business owners and corporate boards run their businesses smoothly and confidently while avoiding risk. I skillfully manage contract issues, shareholder disputes, and I am an aggressive advocate at both the bargaining table and during litigation.

Strategic legal counselor advises on a full range of business issues

For new businesses, I assist with entity formations and filings. I review clients’ business plans and advise on all formation matters to ensure your company’s goals are being met. Once the business is formed, I continue to help it grow by advising on insurance coverage, and corporate compliance issues.

Clients may benefit from my firm’s advice regarding:

  • Business formation and dissolution
  • Buy-sell agreements
  • Contract review and development
  • Due diligence
  • Securities issues

As a knowledgeable business law attorney, I aim to provide thorough, informed guidance for businesses of all sizes and at all stages. I am prepared to handle legal issues of any size or complexity that affect your enterprise. If litigation becomes necessary, you can count on me to work tirelessly to defend your rights in court and provide the best possible results for your case.

Tenacious attorney represents clients in litigation and contract disputes

Conflicts with employees, customers or other companies can disrupt your business operations and affect consumer confidence. When breach of contract or shareholder disputes arise, I will work to resolve them as quickly, efficiently and favorably as possible. If commercial litigation becomes necessary, I will work tirelessly to defend your rights. I am both a seasoned negotiator and litigator who aggressively advocates for your financial interests in the boardroom and the courtroom.
